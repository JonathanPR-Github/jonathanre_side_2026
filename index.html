"THE FIRST TRIES"

The First Tries, we're the first batch of maps i created and released around 2018, at that time i was 15 years old.

Beginning around 2017 and ending late in 2018 during a time when i made these in my free time after school, i created 5 quite simple maps in the world builder for the Source Engine called "Hammer", that initially begun as curiouseness and shortly fell into being fun. This was about the first time when i realized how fun i found development to be, as throughout the time i made them, i found making them more enjoyable for whatever i was doing during that time at school

Unfortently however it would take me quite some time to realize that i should have put most of my work into building a career, so for must of that time i was only curious

Prior to creating these 5 maps, at which a time i saw nothing too the engine, i was making small levels with friends in Roblox based around things i played on that platform, but these we're merely early attempts

Hello, Welcome to Jonathan Erik Reppen's Portfolio

ABOUT ME

I'm a self thought game developer, that takes whatever opportunity is avalible around places and strifes to develop on my own and others sectors to eventually get into the industry

I have for many years been modding several video games, like Star Wars Battlefront, STALKER, and more. But my biggest projects we're within Map making for Team Fortress 2, and Mod making for Heart's of Iron 4, where i was involved in community projects and standalone total overhaul mods

Ever since i started to see the potential i had as a game developer since i started all the way back in 2016 when i was 15, i've been actively developing myself to further enchance my chances of getting a job as a game developer, and as a project to one day produce a video game. In that time, i have been plenty of visits to game studio's like, 2K studio's, been to interviews with game development studio's and developed a mobile game as per a group effort. Even as i had and was recovering from a heart surgery, i contiuned to develop myself to eventually strike gold whenever i could be hired

One thing i've learned as a developer, is to adapt to all kinds of new technologies that comes your way, and that includes Artifical Integellence that i use reguarly to enchance my coding experiences, and to reduce workflow. The overall usage of it has increased the flow by 80% when i work on projects, and that is a very good saying. I normally also tend to work 50/50 with it, with me coding some of the code, and me guiding the artifical intellegence to do it for me.

CURRENT SITUATION

As of April of 2026, i am currently on the bounds to quit the Source engine and move on to fully learn both Unity and Unreal in a gradual amount of time, with Courses and self learning

With the goal of helping me get better chances to get hired in any kind of circumstance.

The second goal, and the thing i will be normally doing alongside it, is developing a video game. Whetever i succed depends on my own abilities as i learn the engines, and the determination i put into it

ABOUT THE TIMELINE

The Timeline is a few sections and rows of development i've done throughout my career, in it you will find a set of mods i made to video games and projects that i've been involved within the last few years alongside some other mixed elements of what i've worked on.

HOW TO INTERACT

To interact with the timeline, find a section listed on the page, hover over one of the boxes/images on the side sections, then click on them.

As soon as you've clicked on them, a popup message will tell you about the project/mod/game was related too, with information and video descriptions about it

You can close the popup message by clicking the X on top of the side of the popup message
